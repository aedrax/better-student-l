BE READY. BE THERE. Suicide Prevention Program

What: Suicide Prevention Program When: Thursday, Sept 10 at 7pm Where: Mac Activities Suicide is the 2nd leading cause of death in the U.S. for individuals ages 15-24. Over 41,000 suicides are reported annually, making it the 10th leading cause of death for Americans. It is tragic to consider how many of these suicides could have been prevented. Would you know what to say? Will you be the one that helps? Join Phi Mu Delta Fraternity and the Counseling Center's Dr. Mike Schafer and Rebecca Diller on Thursday, Sept 10 at 7pm in Mac Activities to learn the risk factors for suicide, identify signs of personal crisis, and understand how to get distressed individuals the help they need. Be Ready. Be There. For questions regarding this event, please contact Andrew Ziegler ( a-ziegler.1@onu.edu). -- *Andrew D. The Prayer Bus is Coming to Town

Dear ONU Campus Community, The National Prayer Bus Tour will be making a stop in Ada on September 17th and 18th. During the day on the 18th, the members of this prayer team will be spending time listening to the prayer concerns of students, faculty and staff on campus and offering prayer for them. If you would like to meet with members of the prayer team, please stop by the Chapel sanctuary between 9 a.m. and 1 p.m. on Friday, September 18th. Then at 1:00 p.m. we will be have a special send off prayer time for the team in the Chapel prayer garden. All interested members of the ONU community are invited to join us at that time. The National Prayer Bus Tour is organized by the National Day of Prayer Task Force, and is being brought to our campus through the efforts of a generous ONU alum. The tour theme this year is Education, and they will be stopping at college and university campuses across the country to offer prayer and encouragement to students and educators at both private and public institutions. ONU is one of only two universities in Ohio where the bus tour will be stopping--the other stop is at Ohio State. You are welcome to join the prayer team for any part (or all) of their visit! If you have any questions, please e-mail me at: d-macdonald@onu.edu Blessings, Chaplain David

Phi Mu Alpha Sinfonia: Rush Week!Sept 14-18

Phi Mu Alpha Sinfonia Fall 2015 Rush Week is set to take place from Monday, September 14th to Friday, September 18th!
