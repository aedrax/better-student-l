BE READY. BE THERE. Suicide Prevention Program

Suicide is the 2nd leading cause of death in the U.S. for individuals ages 15- 24. Over 41,000 suicides are reported annually, making it the 10th leading cause of death for Americans. It is tragic to consider how many of these suicides could have been prevented. Would you know what to say? Will you be the one that helps? Join Phi Mu Delta Fraternity and the Counseling Center's Dr. Mike Schafer and Rebecca Diller on Thursday, Sept 10 at 7pm in Mac Activities to learn the risk factors for suicide, identify signs of personal crisis, and understand how to get distressed individuals the help they need. Be Ready. Be There. What: Suicide Prevention Program When: Thursday, Sept 10 at 7pm Where: Mac Activities For questions regarding this event, please contact Andrew Ziegler (a-ziegler. 1@onu.edu). -- *Andrew D.
