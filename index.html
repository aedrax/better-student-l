These shirts would be the perfect Christmas presents for sisters, brothers, moms, dads, aunts, uncles, cousins, girlfriends, or just your friends from back home and will be here in plenty of time for Christmas! Tetanus kills one newborn every 11 minutes but this is actually a very preventable disease when women of child bearing age are given a very affordable and widely accessible vaccination. Since Circle K and Kiwanis has partnered with UNICEF, we have immunized more than 130 million women in over 53 countries and have completely eliminated the disease in 37 countries.
