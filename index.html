  • 114 DOWN, 86 TO GO!!!

    Hi everyone, As I'm sure you have seen Circle K is selling shirts to raise money to eliminate maternal and neonatal tetanus. So far we have* sold 114* shirts and our *goal is 200*! Please help us meet that goal! Tetanus kills one newborn every 11 minutes but this is actually a very preventable disease when women of child bearing age are given a very affordable and widely accessible vaccination. Since Circle K and Kiwanis has partnered with UNICEF, we have immunized more than 130 million women in over 53 countries and have completely eliminated the disease in 37 countries. Even with so much progress it still remains a major public health risk for many countries. We would love to have your contribution to help eliminate maternal and neonatal tetanus!   • NOTICE TO OFF CAMPUS STUDENTS

    If you have moved off campus, please be sure and place a Change of Address with anyone that may send you items. We are still receiving a considerable amount of mail and packages. Although we will forward any First Class items for a period of one academic year, you will still need to place a Change of Address with anyone that may send you something who still has your previous campus address and unit number. Items received for anyone that has been off campus longer then one academic year, will be returned to the sender. This is an attempt to reduce the delivery time of items addressed to you. Please be sure and notify all parties that may still be using your old address. This could include but may not be limited to, your bank, credit card company, department stores, Amazon.com and many others. Please notify the ONU Mail Center if you have any questions. -- *"Service Is Our Focus"* Thank you, Bill Kent Ohio Northern University Mail Center Manager ONU Recycling Manager ext. 2406
