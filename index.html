Football FenXI

Come join the sisters of Alpha Xi Delta raise funds and awareness for Autism Speaks in their first ever Football FrenXi. Football FrenXi is a friendly flag football tournament broken down into three different brackets - men, women, and co-ed. Registration will be $36 per team of 6 members. If you can not complete a team we will have substitute sisters to help complete the teams. Each team member will have a complimentary t-shirt included! Even if participating is not for you we have an option of buying just the t-shirt for $15. We would love to see you all out on the Tundra supporting those participants and the sisters of Alpha Xi Delta raise funds and awareness for Autism Speaks! Registration forms will be available at the Alpha Xi Delta House, in the Mathile building, and Mac Tables August 31-September 4, as well as at the event during check in at 11:30 a.m. Online registration will also be available. We hope to see you all there! -- Tiffany C. Kline Ohio Northern University Class of 2017 Public Relations/Communication Alpha Xi Delta, Publicity Chair PRSSA Firm Assistant Club Softball

Rush Week! Alpha Phi Omega- Coed Service Fraternity

Hello Ohio Northern Students, Alpha Phi Omega (APhiO) is an international, coed service fraternity with nearly 400 chapters.
